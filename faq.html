Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our CCTV installation, maintenance, and security solutions.

General Questions

We offer a wide range of CCTV cameras including:

  • HD and 4K resolution cameras
  • Indoor and outdoor cameras
  • Night vision cameras
  • PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) cameras
  • Wireless IP cameras
  • Dome cameras

Our experts will help you choose the right cameras based on your specific security needs and budget.

Installation time varies depending on the size of your property and the number of cameras being installed. A typical residential installation can be completed in 1-2 days, while larger commercial installations may take 2-5 days. We'll provide you with a specific timeframe during your consultation.

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranties on both our products and installation work:

  • 2-5 year manufacturer warranty on cameras and equipment
  • 12-month warranty on installation work
  • Extended warranty options available

Technical Questions

Our CCTV systems come with mobile apps and web interfaces that allow you to:

  • View live footage from any device
  • Access recorded footage
  • Receive instant alerts
  • Control PTZ cameras remotely

We'll set up remote access during installation and show you how to use all features.

Storage requirements depend on several factors:

  • Number of cameras
  • Recording resolution
  • Frame rate
  • Recording schedule (24/7 vs motion-activated)
  • Retention period needed

We'll help you calculate the optimal storage capacity during your consultation.

Maintenance Questions

We recommend:

  • Basic systems: Annual maintenance check
  • Commercial systems: Bi-annual maintenance
  • High-security systems: Quarterly maintenance

Regular maintenance ensures optimal performance and extends system life.

Our maintenance service includes:

  • Physical inspection of all cameras
  • Cleaning of camera lenses
  • Check and adjust camera angles
  • Software updates
  • Storage system check
  • Network connection verification
  • System optimization
